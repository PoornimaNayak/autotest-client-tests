NASM is the Netwide Assembler, a free portable assembler for the Intel
80x86 microprocessor series, using primarily the traditional Intel
instruction mnemonics and syntax.

ndisasm - The Netwide Disassembler is a small companion program to the Netwide
Assembler, NASM. The Netwide Disassembler does nothing except to produce
disassemblies of binary source files.

How to run test
---------------
./nasm.sh
